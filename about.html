About the Company

With 25 years of experience in bathroom fittings and accessories, Laxmi Agencies has built a good reputation in exceeding customer expectations and constantly providing quality products of various brands. Since 1988 Laxmi Agencies has continuously evolved in providing best ever services and highly qualified products to its customers. Our products range from traditional to contemporary designs that suit the latest trend of interiors. In addition, we are proud to announce that we have a successful 250 Dealers network all around Telangana and Andhra Pradesh, covering all the important places in these two States. We have proved our ability in delivering best of our services you can trust. It is not only us but it is our dealers around the two states who have helped us come to such a huge position. Nothing defines achieving success more than giving to people in need, and seeing the appreciation and the hope that assistance provides. At the end, it is all about lasting relationships of us and our dealers.
We are also happy to announce that we have a new Retail outlet at Ranigunj.
